PRE RELEASE FAQ

What is Trident Roleplay?

Trident Roleplay is a combat focused RP Server for Arma 3. As a server we are pushing for a strong friendly structured community where everyone can come and have fun.

What custom features does the server have?

The server includes a variety of custom features, mainly including; Casino Games, Gang Cartels, Warzones, Custom Runs and Custom Majors.

What are the requirements for playing on the server?

We will require the basic requirements for all roleplay servers, including having a good quality, clear microphone. Being mature and having a stable internet connection.

What are the server specs?

Intel Core i7-7700 CPU, 64GB DDR4 RAM, 512GB NVMe, 1 Gbit NIC, our servers are DDoS protected and have strong security.

Do donators get any advantage?

All donator perks are purely cosmetic and give no benefit over regular players. We do note accept money for unbans either (cheaters included).

How do I join the Trident Discord?

You can join our discord by clicking the discord logo which can be found in the header and footer of this page.

How do I donate?

Thank you for showing intrest in supporting the server, we plan to have membership packages and gang packages in the future, for standard donations head to the donation page located in the header.

How do I join the team?

We are not currently looking for staff members before release but are open to developers who want to contribute, please contact one of the founders in discord for further information.
